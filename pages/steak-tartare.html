Steak Tartare

Description

Steak Tartare is a dish prepared using raw ground / minced beef and egg yolk. A popular dish in Europe, this recipe is a good stepping stone into the world of raw meats, organs and pates.

Ingredients

  • Ground beef (lean)
  • Eggs
  • Onions
  • Capers
  • Cucumber (or pickled cucumber)

Optional Ingredients

  • Parsley
  • Ketchup
  • Mustard
  • Worcestershire sauce

Steps

  1. Dice the onions.
  2. Slice the cucumber.
  3. Rinse, drain and chop the capers.
  4. Chop the ground beef into smaller pieces.
  5. Add chopped ground beef to a bowl and mix it with hands.
  6. Add ingredients and optional ingredients, if any, and mix further.
  7. Separate egg yolk from the whites.
  8. Roll the ground beef mixture into a ball, and flatten it, leaving a concavity up.
  9. Add egg on top of the ground beef, to rest inside the concavity hole.
  10. Ready to serve, may add salt if desired.
