Crab Ceviche

Description

This is wonderful as an appetizer with tortilla chips or served on tostadas with a thin layer of mayonnaise for a nice refreshing lunch.

Ingredients

  • 1 (8 ounce) package imitation crabmeat, flaked
  • 1 tablespoon of olive oil
  • 2 large tomatoes, chopped
  • 3 serrano peppers, finely chopped
  • 1 red onion, chooped
  • half a bunch of cilantro, chopped
  • 2 limes, juiced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps

  1. Place the imitation crabmeat flakes in a bowl.
  2. Stir in olive oil until every piece is well coated.
  3. Stir in the tomatoes, serrano peppers, onion, and the cilantro.
  4. Squeeze lime juice on top and mix well.
  5. Season generoulsy with salt and pepper.
  6. Cover and refrigerate for about 1 hour before serving.
